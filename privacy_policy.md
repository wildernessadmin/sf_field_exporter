Privacy Policy for Salesforce Field Exporter
Last Updated: March 6, 2026

1. Overview
Salesforce Field Exporter is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is a client-side tool designed to help Salesforce professionals export metadata. It operates directly within your browser and interacts solely with your authenticated Salesforce session.

2. Data Collection and Usage
No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII).

No External Data Transmission: No metadata, field labels, or Salesforce data ever leaves your local machine. All processing happens within the extension's local environment, and files are saved directly to your local storage.

Authentication: The extension utilizes your existing Salesforce browser cookies to authenticate API requests. We do not have access to your Salesforce username or password.

3. Permissions Justification
activeTab: Used only to detect the current Salesforce object you are viewing to provide contextual export options.

cookies: Used to access the session ID (sid) required to communicate with the Salesforce REST API.

downloads: Used to save the generated CSV file to your device.

Host Permissions (*.salesforce.com, *.force.com): Required to allow the extension to retrieve metadata from your specific Salesforce organization.

4. Third-Party Services
If enabled in the source code, a link to "Buy Me a Coffee" is provided for optional donations. Clicking this link will take you to a third-party website with its own privacy policy.

5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/wildernessadmin/sf_field_exporter.
